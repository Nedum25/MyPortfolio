About Me

I'm Chinedu Uzim, an AI Researcher, Data Scientist, and Frontend Developer passionate about building intelligent systems that solve real-world problems. I develop user interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I build AI-driven applications and data pipelines using Python, R, SQL, and Power BI.

I'm currently pursuing a PhD in the United Kingdom, researching causal, predictive, and proactive AI systems for sustainable food security in Sub-Saharan Africa. My work focuses on designing AI-driven decision support systems that connect climate variability and agricultural data to actionable policy recommendations. I am also the creator of SmartSkan — an intelligent symptom-to-scan recommendation system featured by Skannr.com.
